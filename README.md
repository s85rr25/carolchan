# Professor Carol Kwai Kuen Chan - Academic Portfolio Website

A modern, professional portfolio website for **Professor Carol Kwai Kuen Chan**, Honorary Professor at the Faculty of Education, University of Hong Kong.

## 📋 Overview

This portfolio website showcases Professor Chan's distinguished career in learning sciences, knowledge building, and computer-supported collaborative learning (CSCL). The site features:

- **Hero Section** with research profile and Google Scholar metrics (h-index: 40, Citations: 6,668+)
- **About/Biography** with three interconnected research areas
- **Education Timeline** (Bachelor's to Ph.D.)
- **Career Positions** (1993–present)
- **Awards & Honors** (9+ major awards including British Academy Fellowship)
- **Selected Publications** (10 journal articles + 2 books, with Google Scholar integration)
- **Editorial Roles** (Associate Editor IJCSCL, Editorial Board member for 4 journals)
- **Research Grants** (4 major funded projects)
- **Keynote Presentations** (6 international keynotes)
- **Graduate Supervision** (17 Ph.D./Ed.D. graduates supervised)
- **Contact Information** with multiple channels
